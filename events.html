01
Equity Pitch Challenge
Analyse · Research · Pitch
Research Competition
The Equity Research Competition is the flagship Finance & Investment event of the Finance and Economics Summit , IIT Ropar. The objective is to develop a comprehensive equity research report that evaluates a company’s business model, financial performance, industry positioning, and future growth potential. Participants are required to analyze financial statements, assess valuation using relevant methodologies, identify key drivers and risks, and arrive at a clear investment thesis supported by data and logic.
Team Size
2–3 members
Format
PPT(max. 10 slides) + 10-min pitch

Groww Workshop —
Markets, Live.

An exclusive hands-on session by the Groww Invest Tech Pvt. Ltd. Groww is bringing AIKG Youth Code to IIT Ropar. What students can expect from this session is a grassroot level break down about capital markets, investing and related research.

1. How the stock market works? Beyond definitions and charts, discussions about what drives prices and how money flows.
2. How to read company financials? Detailed discussion about how to break down balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and IPO documents to figure out genuine potential.
3. How to evaluate if a stock is worth your money? Understand the basics of fundamental analysis - what to look for? what to ignore? and how to identify companies with real long-term potential?
4. Ask anything, no-filter Q&A. Whether you’re starting from zero or already experimenting, get your questions answered directly by an expert who has spent a long time in the capital markets.
