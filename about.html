About FES 2026

The first-ever Finance & Economics Summit at IIT Ropar — our mission, our pillars, and why this is just the beginning.

Our Story

A new chapter for finance at IIT Ropar

We're proud to introduce FES 2026 — our campus's first-ever Finance and Economics Summit. Built to deepen the finance culture at IIT Ropar by connecting students with industry professionals, real-world challenges, and meaningful competition.

From expert speaker sessions and a Hands-on workshop powered by Groww to high-stakes competitions — FES is where ambition meets opportunity. Built by students, for students.

We believe that finance education shouldn't end in the classroom. FES 2026 is our commitment to bridging the gap between academic theory and industry practice — creating a platform where the next generation of finance professionals can learn, compete, and connect.

Our Values

What drives FES 2026

01
Industry Access
Breaking down the barrier between campus and the world of finance. We bring practitioners directly to students — not just for talks, but for real conversations and lasting connections.
02
Applied Learning
Finance is best learned by doing. Every competition and workshop at FES is designed to simulate the real-world pressures and decisions that finance professionals face every day.
03
Community Building
FES 2026 is the beginning of something lasting. We're not just running an event — we're building a finance culture at IIT Ropar that will grow with every passing year.
